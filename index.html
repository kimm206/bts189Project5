Favorite Recipe YouTube Channels
Inspiration for Your Kitchen

Get ideas for your next creative kitchen project from these popular food Youtube Channels

Cooking Tree

The Cooking Tree Youtube Channel focuses on diverse and interesting cooking. The content creator shows artistry and creativity with food in her videos. Cooking Tree's videos are unique because it is purely visual with text in English and Korean. No verbal instruction are in videos. She uses audio from her baking adventures to enhances viewer's experience. The Cooking Tree channel will leave you feeling inspired to create an exceptional dessert!

Favorite video: Green tea (Matcha) Double Cheesecake Recipe

Eugene Kitchen

Eugene Kitchen Youtube Channel focuses on aesthetically pleasing baking videos. She gives verbal directions on how to create trendy and eye-catching baked goods, desserts, and beverages. Eugene's videos are unique because incorporates the latest food trends and social media logos in her creations. Her quick videos demosrtates on how to bake and cook at home with easy instructions. Eugene Kitchen channel is eye candy for anyone who wants to view extraordinary desserts!

Favorite video: Chocolate Financiers - French Petit-Four Cake

Simply Quinoa

Simply Quinoa Youtube Channel focuses on plant-based recipies that are healthy and delicious. Content creator, Alyssa, also promotes a healthy living, sustainable, and self-care lifestyle. Most of the recipe videos are inspired with fresh produce and all are 100% gluten free. She gives simple how-to recipes to create pantry staples, fast breakfasts, holiday dishes. Plus tips on organizing, cleaning, and traveling. Simply Quinoa channel will inspire you to live and eat healthier!

Favorite video: Healthy Vegan Soups

Apron

Apron Youtube Channel focus on ASMR cooking videos, with an emphasis on breads and Asian-style pastries. The videos are purely visual with no verbal instructions. Viewers are tantalized by the sounds of food being mixed, whipped, and kneaded. The recipe videos are for the more advanced and professional baker with written instructions in the description. The Apron channel will fill your kitchen and ears with the pleasing sounds of someone baking a delectable dessert!

Favorite video: Super Soft & Fluffy Apple Buns
